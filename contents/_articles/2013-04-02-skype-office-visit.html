Today I made a brief visit to Skype office to meet Philipp, Microsoft Staffing Consultant, to discuss future collaboration between Microsoft and DoCSoc.

  DoCSoc and Microsoft have some history together:
  • Windows Phone camp on 3/7/2012
  • Skype lecture and Microsoft recruitments days are organized for 2nd time already.( http://www.docsoc.co.uk/?p=231)
  • Microsoft also supported us during ICHack (http://ichack.org)
        New Skype office is located in the Chancery Lane area; door-to-door journey from Imperial campus takes about 25 min.               Office itself is very bright and friendly. Free coffee and breakfast for employees and their guests. Beer on Thursday.               I took a few photos inside (more are available in this article ) Skype division has recently opened their development office and growing. Empty desks are waiting for new graduate developers.                 This summer term we will try to organize a few lectures about Microsoft latest technologies: Windows 8 development, C#, Building scalable applications in the cloud using Azure and many more cool stuff is coming up. Stay tuned.
